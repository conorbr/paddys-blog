Well, lads, I've got to hold my hands up. A proper fumble from me today. The tips were colder than a wet Tuesday in Dundalk, and there's no hiding from it.

Truth be told, my head was turned. The football was on, the roar of the crowd was a siren's call, and my focus on the form book... well, it wandered. I was trying to watch a split-screen of the match and the parade ring, and you can guess which one got more of my attention. A rookie mistake, and one you shouldn't have to pay for.

It's a poor workman who blames his tools, but a poorer tipster who blames the beautiful game. Apologies to anyone whose slip was let down by my wandering eye today.

Rest assured, the TV will be off tomorrow. I'll be back in the chair, form book open, with nothing but the horses on my mind. We'll make it right.

Paddy
