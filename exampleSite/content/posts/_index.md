### Filing a post (categories & tags)

**Categories** — pick what fits best (one primary; a second only if it really applies):

| Category | Use it for |
|----------|------------|
| **Racing** | Regular cards, UK & Ireland, days in front of the telly. |
| **Festivals** | Big-meeting weeks (Cheltenham, Aintree, Galway, Punchestown, etc.). |
| **Notebook** | Short pieces — single race, quick angle, form note. |
| **Opinion** | Stewards, riding, industry — clear armchair takes. |

**Tags** — add any that apply (helps readers and search):

`irish-racing`, `uk-racing`, `jumps`, `flat`, `cheltenham`, `handicaps`, `stewards`, `watch-along`, `antepost`, `introduction`, …

In the post front matter:

```yaml
categories: ["Racing"]
tags: ["uk-racing", "jumps", "watch-along"]
```

The **Documentation** category is for old **LoveIt theme demos**; new writing uses the four categories above.
