So the young fella set me up with this website. I told him I only wanted a place to write down what I'm thinking when the **racing** is on — same as I'd talk to yourself in the kitchen, except now it's on the **internet**, God help us.

I'll be posting when there's something worth saying: a horse that stayed on when the others folded, a ride I can't fathom, or a Saturday on the **telly** that felt like a festival day. Take it all with a pinch of salt; I'm not tipping for a living, I'm enjoying the game.

Slán for now — the next is going to post in a few minutes and I need the remote.
